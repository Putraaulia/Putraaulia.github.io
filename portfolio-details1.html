  • Category: Apps design
  • Client: Bank Riau Kepri
  • Project date: 21 March, 2021

Sistem Informasi E-Saleskit berbasis Mobile

Digital Sales Kit is a tool that helps Bank Riau Kepri employees to provide information about products and services to customers. The previous version of the Digital Sales Kit only contained information about conventional banks. Meanwhile, Bank Riau Kepri has recently transformed into a Shariah Commercial Bank. This prompted the Service Quality Desk to take the initiative to develop the latest Digital Sales Kit product, of course with materials that are easily updated and take advantage of the latest technological developments. In this way, the users responsible for controlling this function can easily update the information materials according to the development and addition of Bank Riau Kepri's service products.
